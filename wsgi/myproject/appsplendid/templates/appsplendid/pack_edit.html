Unqiue symbols

To make a pack we need to have unqiue symbols. A symbol can be made from a word, a group of words or an image. For example:-
A list of 4 word or words: Merci, S'il vous plaît, Au revoir, Ça va?\nHow are you
Images can be saved in a zip file and then uploaded.

How Many Unqiue symbols

Unique symbols needed depends upon how many symbols per card you wish to have in your pack.

  • 3 symbols per cards, a total of 7 or more unique symbols are needed
  • 4 symbols per cards, a total of 13 or more unique symbols are needed
  • 5 symbols per cards, a total of 21 or more unique symbols are needed
  • 6 symbols per cards, a total of 31 or more unique symbols are needed
  • 7 symbols per cards, a total of 43 or more unique symbols are needed
  • 8 symbols per cards, a total of 57 or more unique symbols are needed
